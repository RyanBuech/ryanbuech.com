Depths Of Madness
ABOUT THIS GAME
Depths of Madness is an atmospheric horror game. Players will explore the underwater facility, collect audio logs to learn about the incident, the entity called Cthulhu, and what happened after his discovery. Players will find key cards to unlock the facilities locked doors and plant the explosives they brought with them to bring the whole facility down. It won't be so easy however, Cthulhu will chase the player, listen for the players activity, and hunt them down once he finds them.
Features:
A dynamic and intelligent A.I.
Atmospheric lighting and level design
Fully voiced audio logs featuring seven voice actors
Custom audio and soundtrack
My Work On This Game:
On Depths of Madness I was responsible for the majority of the UX and UI.
I handled all of the game menus and audio playing throughout the game.
The game had alot of audio easter eggs, since the project was in Unity I was able to make alot of features and prefabs that made placement and audio selection of these easter eggs easy for our designers.
